Awesome! You're here!

This November we will bring you RocketConf, a three-day developer conference for anyone who loves writing beatiful code and creating magnificent things that matter.

Over the course of three days you may participate in one of the workshops, and enjoy a diverse selection of talks during the two-day, single-track conference days.

Want us to keep you up to date on new speakers, ticket sales and other important things? Follow @RocketConf or subscribe to our mailinglist.
