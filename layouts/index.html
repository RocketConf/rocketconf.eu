Share with us! Call for Proposals

RocketConf 2015 targets developers working with modern web development tools in the broad sense of the word. Ranging from front-end JavaScript to back-end infrastructure and everything in between.

In the light of our Code of Conduct we want to give everyone a fair change of speaking at RocketConf 2015. Our programme team, making the speaker selection, will not have access to speaker details until they need to start contacting people.

Please submit your proposal for a talk or workshop before 1 August 2015.
